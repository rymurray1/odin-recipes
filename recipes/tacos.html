Delicious Bacon and Avocado Breakfast Tacos

Breakfast tacos are a fun and easy way to start your morning. There is nothing better than waking up on a saturday and making yourself some nice breakfast tacos! They can be good and healthy if you pick the right recipe and ingredients. And always remember to get the best tortillas available

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh smoked bacon
  • 2 eggs per person
  • 1/2 avocado per person
  • Serrano hot sauce from Yellowbird
  • Mexican blend cheese, or Oxacan if Mexican blend is unavailable
  • Optional: Grilled white onion

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon over the stove or in the oven, whichever is more convenient.
  2. Next, warm up your tortillas. This is essential for a good taco.
  3. Finally, add the rest of the ingredients to your taco to assemble.
